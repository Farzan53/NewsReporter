Champions Trophy: Indian Team Refuses to Display Pakistan’s Name on Jerseys

In a widely expected but still discourteous move, India will reportedly forgo the custom of wearing the host country’s name on their jerseys during the upcoming 2025 Champions Trophy. The ICC-designed logo for the 2025 Champions Trophy prominently features the host country, Pakistan. However, reports from Indian media claim that the Indian cricket team will not display Pakistan’s name on their jerseys during the tournament.
